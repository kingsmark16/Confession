# Pop Art Confession Design

source: image and HTML export from the supplied Stitch screen

## Character

Loud, handwritten, romantic pop art. The page uses a yellow comic dot field, black ink outlines, rough sticker geometry, strong red and green hits, and oversized playful lettering.

## Build mandate

Keep the screen full, expressive, and easy to scan. Use strong black borders, offset hard shadows, slight rotation, visible motion, and generous vertical rhythm. Preserve the exact section order from the Stitch screen. Use plain semantic HTML and respect reduced motion preferences.

## Composition

Fixed white navigation, full hero, centered love letter, four photo memories, three reasons cards, final response prompt, and black footer.

## Responsive behavior

The hero and content scale down on narrow screens. Navigation links and decorative hero stickers hide when space is tight. Memory cards become one column, then two columns, then four columns as space allows.

## Tokens

The source of truth for colors, type, spacing, shadows, borders, and motion is `src/index.css`.
